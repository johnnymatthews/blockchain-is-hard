Why this site exists...

It seems that there's far too much complicated jargon and acryonyms when it comes to blockchain development. So we've put together this site to help explain some of the concepts as simply as possible. There's two sections to this site. Glossary is where you'll find basic definitions of words or phrases. Concepts is where you can read longer posts that discuss how stuff works, or why certain things happen.
