  • Address

    Imagine it's your birthday and your friend wants to send you a birthday card. They need to know what your house address is before they can send you the card. Addresses work the same way in Aion. If you friend wants to send you some AION, they need to know your address. Each address is unique, and is 66 characters long. Every single Aion address starts with 0xa.

  • dApp

    A decentralized application. Regular applications like Netflix and Wikipedia generally have two points of contact, your computer and their servers.
    This is what happens when you want to view an article on Wikipedia:

    1. You click on a link to view an article.
    2. The Wikipedia receives your request.
    3. It searches for the article.
    4. It send the article back to you.
    5. Your computer shows it on the screen.

    This is how the majority of the internet works. You request something from a server, and the server sends it back to you. There are a lot of problems with this method. For example, if someone wanted you to see an edited version of the article, all they have to do is change it on the Wikipedia server.
    In a Dapp the user asks a network of computers for the article, and they collectively respond with all the data. This way the user can be 100% sure that the article they are getting back is completely correct, since every single computer on the network has to send the exact same article. If one of the computers tries to change the article, all the other computers will disagree and kick that computer from the network.

  • Fiat Currency

    Fiat currency is physical things people use to trade. United States dollars are fiat currency, as is gold and silver. Cigarettes are often used in prison as fiat currency. It is essentially just something that a collection of people regard as having value.
    For more of a history on fiat currency, check out Extra Credit's History of Money series.

  • Fungible

    Having two things that are exactly the same in value. A single $10 bill has exactly the same value as two $5 bills. It's the same with most cryptocurrencies. My Bitcoin (1BTC) has the exact same value as your Bitcoin (1BTC). Another word for this is interchangeable.
    The opposite of this is non-fungible, which means the opposite. Non-fungible is when two things don't have the same value. My bottle of wine is worth $30, but yours is older and from a more prestigious vineyard, so it's worth $100.

  • Genesis Block

    This is the very first block on a blockchain. When a builder is building a wall, they must place the first brick and secure it in place before moving onto the next brick. When the builder has finished building the wall, every single brick on the wall can be traced back to the original brick.
    In a similar was, every single block on the Aion blockchain can be traced back to the genesis block. Knowing where the genesis block is, or what was on it, isn't really important for day to day Dapp development.
